Our story · Singapore, since 2024

Real food, real care —
made by people who love it.

Vegely started in a small Tiong Bahru kitchen with one belief: healthy eating should never feel like a compromise on taste, design, or convenience.

Our story

From a home kitchen to all of Singapore.

In 2024, our founder Aisha was juggling a startup job, a half-marathon programme, and the endless quest for a lunch that wouldn't wreck either. She started cooking weekly meal-preps for friends — and within a month, the WhatsApp group had grown from 4 friends to 140 strangers.

Today, Vegely is a full Singapore healthy-food delivery service: chef-crafted bowls, farm-to-bowl produce, nutritionist-balanced macros, and a delivery network that reaches every postcode on the island — usually within 30 minutes of leaving our kitchen.

Our mission

To make healthy eating the easiest, tastiest choice in Singapore.

We believe wellness shouldn't feel like punishment. Every Vegely meal is built around three non-negotiables — locally sourced ingredients, balanced macros, and joyful flavour — so eating well becomes the obvious daily choice, not the difficult one.
